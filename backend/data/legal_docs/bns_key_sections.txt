BHARATIYA NYAYA SANHITA (BNS) 2023 - KEY SECTIONS

Section 63 - Rape
Defines rape and its various forms. Punishment: Rigorous imprisonment not less than 10 years, extendable to life imprisonment and fine.

Section 64 - Punishment for Rape
Minimum 10 years RI, may extend to life. If victim under 16: minimum 20 years. If victim under 12: minimum 20 years or life or death.

Section 65 - Rape in certain cases
Rape by police officer, public servant, armed forces - minimum 10 years RI extendable to life.

Section 100 - Murder
Culpable homicide is murder if done with intention to cause death, or with intention to cause bodily injury likely to cause death, or with knowledge that act is likely to cause death.

Section 101 - Punishment for Murder
Death or life imprisonment and fine.

Section 103 - Punishment for murder by life convict
Death penalty.

Section 105 - Culpable Homicide not amounting to Murder
If act done with knowledge likely to cause death but without intention - up to 10 years and fine.

Section 109 - Attempt to Murder
If hurt caused: up to life imprisonment and fine. If no hurt: up to 10 years and fine.

Section 115 - Voluntarily causing hurt
Imprisonment up to 1 year or fine up to Rs 10,000 or both.

Section 117 - Voluntarily causing grievous hurt
Imprisonment up to 7 years and fine.

Section 118 - Voluntarily causing hurt by dangerous weapons
Imprisonment up to 3 years or fine or both.

Section 121 - Causing hurt by acid attack
Minimum 10 years RI extendable to life and fine.

Section 123 - Dowry death
If death within 7 years of marriage and subjected to cruelty - minimum 7 years RI extendable to life.

Section 140 - Kidnapping
Kidnapping from lawful guardianship or from India.

Section 141 - Kidnapping for ransom
Punishment: Death or life imprisonment and fine.

Section 143 - Wrongful confinement
Imprisonment up to 1 year or fine up to Rs 1000 or both.

Section 174 - Robbery
Theft with violence or threat of violence.

Section 175 - Dacoity
When 5 or more persons commit robbery jointly.

Section 303 - Theft
Dishonest removal of movable property - up to 3 years imprisonment or fine or both.

Section 304 - Snatching
Theft by snatching - imprisonment up to 3 years and fine.

Section 305 - Theft in dwelling house
Imprisonment up to 7 years and fine.

Section 308 - Extortion
Putting person in fear to deliver property - imprisonment up to 3 years or fine or both.

Section 309 - Robbery
Theft with force - RI up to 10 years and fine. If on highway between sunset and sunrise: up to 14 years.

Section 310 - Dacoity with murder
Death or life imprisonment with fine.

Section 316 - Criminal Breach of Trust
Imprisonment up to 3 years or fine or both.

Section 318 - Cheating
Imprisonment up to 3 years or fine or both.

Section 319 - Cheating by personation
Imprisonment up to 5 years or fine or both.

Section 329 - Criminal Trespass
Imprisonment up to 3 months or fine up to Rs 5000 or both.

Section 330 - House Trespass
Imprisonment up to 1 year or fine up to Rs 5000 or both.

Section 351 - Criminal Intimidation
Imprisonment up to 2 years or fine or both. If threat to cause death: up to 7 years.

Section 356 - Defamation
Simple imprisonment up to 2 years or fine or both.
